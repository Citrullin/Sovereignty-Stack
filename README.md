# Sovereignty Stack

Decades of technological stagnation. At least this is how Thiel describes it.
He might not be wrong. The irony, his consequence is the destruction of society.
Instead of realizing, technology always had an institutional ego and ignorance towards sociology.
Even though, it explains a lot of the trust and organizational issues we run into, again.
Every generation again, the cycle of humanity. What is the Sovereignty Stack?
The technological framework, template for sovereign entities in the 21th century.
It's so much more than just some IoT tools, Blockchain Smart Contracts and genAI microblock tools.
The world is plaqued from traditional thinking patterns, which have to be broken.
Yet, we cannot throw everything away and should not even try to, as those acceleranoists suggest.
We don't need to destroy everything in order to advance quicker. We only need the right tools.
Bringing functioning systems and society structures onchain and enforcing them with IoT and AI.
Not in the scary suveillance way, but with sovereign entities that own their data and devices.
More importantly, in a world of propaganda, sovereign entities who own their mind. 

The investment thesis, if you will, is very simple:
We live in low-trust multi-polar world with degrading trust left. Trust is the backbone for society.
Focusing on this remaining trust and advancing from there with technology is the best way foward.
Not as fantasy, but pragmatic approach with tools that work now, not in maybe 20 years.
On hardware that is widely accessible to users worldwide. This includes 20 year old recycled thin clients.
We have lived on the achievements of the past and cared more about the perception than the actual status quo.
This shows in degrading large institutions that have been followed out by Greed, Ego and Envy.
Web3/DeFi is just a continuation of this trend, but has the potential to be more than that.
Issue is, we need those sociological (aka institutional) trust structures to operate this advanced economy.
Instead of fighting them, the challenge is to figure out how to enable these structures onchain.
From there, you can still more to your everything is decentralized mentality. Federated just works.
The world is moving in this direction, there is no time to fantasies, when reality is right behind us.
A pragmatic apporach to sovereign. To create resiliant collectives that survive their own original location.
It uses society and therefore sociological mechanisms to create a stronger feeling of us, trust.
The [NFT NFC badge](hardware/tiny-pay/img/NFC_Badge.png), for example, is just technological extension of a [mechanism crypto users already use.](hardware/tiny-pay/img/community_NFT_keychain.jpg)
This is what the Sovereignty Stack does. It observes sociological mechanisms in real life.
Checks what technological solutions are available that match those mechanisms, but extend them.
Extend them with Sovereignty features, such as data ownership or Blockchain accessiblity. 
Web 3 has missed out on this opportunity by looking too much into the past.
It was too comforfable in the US-driven world to not really change, even though you wanted.
The opportunity lays now into bridging federated, decentralized and traditional technology/approaches.
To create a base for culture collectives to build on and anchor their trust on.
