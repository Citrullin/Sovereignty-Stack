From Space Engineering to Bionics

In recent years Origami had some major breakthroughs.
Until the end of the 20th century there were only a limited amount of folds known to mankind.
95% of all folds known today have been invented in the last 20 years.
Origami can truly be called a engineering revolution of the 21th century.
